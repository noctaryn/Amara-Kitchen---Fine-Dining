Our Philosophy

Every dish tells
a story

At Amara, we believe fine dining should feel like coming home. Our chefs draw from the deep wells of Nigerian culinary heritage — egusi, oha, ofada — and reinterpret them through a contemporary lens.

Each ingredient is sourced from local farmers. Each recipe is a conversation between the past and the present.
